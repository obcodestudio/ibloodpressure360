 Description:
     The iBloodPressure360 is a self-monitoring blood pressure application that allows you to browse and archive without easily. The statistics of systolic and diastolic occurrences, percentages, number of incidences during the day and night, and occurrence trends from January to December is made available.
 Functions:
 Low pressure statistics.
 Hypertension reminder.
 The statistics of systolic and diastolic occurrences, percentages, number of incidences during the day and night is made available.
 There are 4 numerical ranges to measure with systolic: normal lower than 120, Pre hypertension between 120-139, s1 hypertension between 140-159 and s2 hypertension higher than 160.
 There are 4 numerical ranges to measure with diastolic: normal lower than 80, Pre hypertension between 80-89, s1 hypertension between 90-99 and s2 hypertension higher than 100.
 There are 3 numerical ranges to measure with pulse: low lower than 60, normal between 60-99, high higher than 100.
 There are 6 occurrence trends to measure with diastolic and systolic.
 To record your systolic, diastolic, pulse rate, important information
 Data transmission with e-mail.
 Export a text file of your blood pressure data if you need a delimited text file to load into another program.
 Archive old data to delete database, so that the app performance can stay optimal.
 Export your blood pressure information directly to Excel (CSV) if you’re not sure what info you need and you’d rather delete and rearrange columns in a spreadsheet program.
 To provide voice input of blood pressure level.
